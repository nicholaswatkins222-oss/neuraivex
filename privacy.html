Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you book a consultation through our website, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. This information is provided voluntarily through our scheduling form (Calendly).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information solely to:

  • Confirm your scheduled consultation
  • Send appointment reminders via phone call or text message
  • Follow up regarding your consultation

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. SMS / Text Messaging

By providing your phone number during the booking process, you consent to receive automated appointment reminder text messages from Neuraivex. Message frequency is approximately 1–2 messages per scheduled appointment.

Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of text messages at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive from us. For help, reply HELP or contact us at nicholas@neuraivex.com.

4. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for the duration of our business relationship and for a reasonable period thereafter. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • Calendly — appointment scheduling
  • Twilio — SMS delivery
  • Airtable — appointment data storage

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:
nicholas@neuraivex.com
