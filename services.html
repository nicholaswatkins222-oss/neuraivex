02
AI-Personalized Outreach

Every email is written to feel personal — because the logic behind it is. We use contextual signals from each prospect's company, role, and recent activity to generate first lines that don't read like templates.

Multi-step sequences handle timing and follow-ups automatically. Reply detection routes interested leads straight to your booking link.

What's included
  • Multi-step email sequences (3–5 touches)

  • Subject line A/B testing
  • Send-time optimization
  • Automated reply detection and routing
  • Bounce and unsubscribe handling
03
Pipeline & Follow-Up Automation

Once a lead shows interest, the system handles everything after. Follow-up sequences, CRM sync, and booking link delivery all run automatically — so no lead goes cold because someone forgot to follow up.

Monthly reporting gives you a clear view of what's working — volume, reply rates, booking rates — so we can continuously optimize targeting and messaging.

What's included
  • Automated follow-up cadences post-reply
  • CRM integration (HubSpot, Airtable, Notion, or Google Sheets)
  • Booking link injection on positive replies
  • Lead status tracking and pipeline visibility
  • Monthly performance reports
  • Ongoing targeting and copy optimization

FAQ

Common questions.

How long does setup take?
7–10 business days from the discovery call. We map your ICP, build the lead list, write the sequences, and get everything wired and tested before we go live.
Do I need to provide a lead list?
No. Lead sourcing is part of the system. We build and maintain the list based on your ICP — you don't need to supply anything except clarity on who you want to reach.
Which CRMs do you support?
We work with most common options — HubSpot, Airtable, Notion, and Google Sheets. If you're using something else, let us know on the call and we'll confirm compatibility.
What if I don't see results?
We rework the targeting and messaging until it converts. Cold outreach performance is heavily dependent on ICP fit and copy quality — both of which we control and optimize continuously.
